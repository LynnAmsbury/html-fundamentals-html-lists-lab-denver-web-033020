• 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • 2 slices of cheddar
    • 1 slice of mozzarella
    • 1 slice of pepper jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter
  1. Spread half of the butter on one side of each slice of bread
  2. Place 1 slice of bread in frying pan buttered side down and fry
  3. Add cheese on top of bread
  4. Cover with the second slice of bread, buttered side up (not touching the cheese)
  5. Turn over and fry for 2 minutes
